# Overwatch Cute Spray Reference

Maintenance reference for the `!ow challenge` board, built from the in-game
Achievements / Hero Challenges data. **51 of 52 current heroes have a Cute spray** —
all are seeded by `[p]ow challenge seedcute`. Only **Shion** (newest, ~June 2026)
has none yet.

Conditions are completable only in **Quick Play or Competitive** (no Arcade).
In-game achievement name is in parentheses.

> Patch-drift note: Orisa's Cute spray moved off her Energy Javelin (now her *Pixel*,
> "Tip of The Spear") onto her Terra Surge ultimate ("Terrable Terrable Damage").
> If any condition ever looks off, the in-game text is the source of truth.
